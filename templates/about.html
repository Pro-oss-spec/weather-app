About Weather App

Your trusted companion for accurate weather information.

Who We Are

Weather App is a modern weather forecasting application that provides users with reliable and real-time weather information. Our goal is to help people plan their day with confidence by delivering clear and accurate weather updates.

Our Mission

We aim to make weather information simple, accessible, and visually appealing. Whether you're planning a trip, heading to work, or just curious about the weather, we've got you covered.

Features

  • Current Weather Conditions
  • 5-Day Weather Forecast
  • Temperature & Humidity
  • Wind Speed
  • Air Pressure
  • Dark Mode Support
